Features

Security and data protection

The very foundation of our service is our proprietary cloud platform. It is built from scratch for high performance and high security to make sure your data is always safe. Coming out of Europe, we strictly follow GDPR, and we will never be forced to reveal any data through the US Cloud act.

True customization and scalability

Regardless if you need one server, or tens of thousands, massive amounts of CPU and RAM, but little storage, or the other way around; With our customization wizard you’ll get exactly the setup you need, not some predefined package others have decided is best for you.

Predictable pricing

At the core of our philosophy is predictable pricing. You may upscale or downscale at your convenience, at any time, and with a flat pricing structure, you’ll never get unexpectedly high bills. We even have unlimited bandwidth included, so there’s no need to worry if your traffic peaks.
